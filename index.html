OmniAvatar

Efficient Audio-Driven Avatar Video Generation with Adaptive Body Animation

Significant progress has been made in audio-driven human animation, while most existing methods focus mainly on facial movements, limiting their ability to create full-body animations with natural synchronization and fluidity. To tackle this issue, we introduce OmniAvatar, an innovative audio-driven full-body video generation model that enhances body animation with improved lip-sync accuracy and natural movements. Our approach includes a pixel-wise multi-hierarchical audio embedding strategy to better capture audio features in the latent space, enhancing lip-syncing across diverse human scenes. We also employ LoRA-based training to maintain the original performance of the foundation models while efficiently integrating audio features. Extensive experiments show that OmniAvatar surpasses existing models in both facial and semi-body video generation, offering precise text-based control for creating videos in various domains, such as podcasts, human interactions, dynamic scenes, and singing.

Generated Videos

OmniAvatar can generate lifelike speaking avatar videos that the characters' actions and expressions are natural and rich, with audio perfectly synchronized to their lip movements. OmniAvatar also supports for controlled movement amplitudes by prompts.

Human-object interaction while speaking

OmniAvatar is able to interact with objects while speaking, significantly broadening the application scenarios for audio-driven digital avatars.
